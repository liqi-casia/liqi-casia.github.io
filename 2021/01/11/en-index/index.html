Personal Profile

Dr. Qi Li received the BSc degree in Automation from China University of Petroleum, and Ph.D degree in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent System from National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition, Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2011 and 2016, respectively. Since July 2016, Dr. Qi Li has joined the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences where he is currently an Assistant Professor. His current research focuses on computer vision, biometrics and face preprocessing etc.

Research Areas

Computer vision, biometrics, face preprocessing
